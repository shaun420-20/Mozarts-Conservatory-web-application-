ABOUT US

The MOZART'S CONSERVATORY beats at the very heart of Mumbai's thriving entertainment business.
WE offer a range of part time & full time courses in Hindustani &
Western classical music & music technology. We offer programmes for all ages & abilities.
From the import of quality instruments & accessories to raising the floor for musicians.
MC strives to continue to uplift the standards of music activity all over the country.
We meet the highest standards of International education & proud to e affiliated to TRINITY COLLEGE OF MUSIC,LONDON .The directors of MC play a pivotal role in steering MC towards its success.

OUR PROGRAMMES

Music is fun and gratifying.All children have the need to express themselves and playing a musical instrument can provide an outlet for creative and emotional expression
Learning the guitar has always been on the withlist of almost every human being at some point in their lives
Mozart's Conservatory offers this beautiful musical journey for age 8 and above. The students will be trained from the beginning and you will see how easily and skilfully they can do magic
Singing comes naturally to most of us. Children connect with music,especially singing ,very easily,as early as listening to a mother's lullaby that lulls them to sleep.
