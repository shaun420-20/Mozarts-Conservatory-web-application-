Description of Programme

The GUITAR is widely employed In all genres of music for solo & ensemble performances.It is one of the most popular instrument for learning instructions at a young age as it develops a child's ability.It is recognized as primary instrument in genres such as a blues,country,jazz,metal,pop


Structure/Duration:

The curriculum is structured in progressive levels of difficulty.The duration for each grade is 20 weeks.Students can work towards taking an examination from TRINITY COLLEGE OF MUSIC,LONDON

Opportunity for performance development include recitals & annual concerts.
